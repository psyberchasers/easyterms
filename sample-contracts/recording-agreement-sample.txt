EXCLUSIVE RECORDING AGREEMENT

This Exclusive Recording Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of March 15, 2024 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

ARTIST: Marcus "MarcyBeats" Johnson, an individual residing at 1234 Music Lane, Los Angeles, CA 90028 (hereinafter referred to as "Artist")

AND

LABEL: Stellar Sound Records, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company with offices at 5678 Sunset Boulevard, Suite 400, Los Angeles, CA 90028 (hereinafter referred to as "Label" or "Company")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Artist is a talented musical artist who performs under the professional name "MarcyBeats"; and

WHEREAS, Label is engaged in the business of producing, manufacturing, distributing, and selling phonograph records and other audio and audiovisual recordings; and

WHEREAS, Label desires to obtain Artist's exclusive recording services, and Artist desires to render such services to Label, upon the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1: TERM

1.1 Initial Period. The initial term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of one (1) year ("Initial Period"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof.

1.2 Option Periods. Label shall have four (4) separate and consecutive options to extend the Term for additional periods of one (1) year each ("Option Periods"). Each Option Period shall automatically commence upon expiration of the preceding contract period unless Label gives written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the expiration of the then-current period.

1.3 Total Term. The Initial Period and any Option Periods exercised are collectively referred to as the "Term."

ARTICLE 2: RECORDING COMMITMENT

2.1 Minimum Recording Commitment. During each contract period, Artist agrees to record and deliver to Label sufficient Master Recordings to constitute the following:

    (a) Initial Period: One (1) Album containing a minimum of twelve (12) Master Recordings
    (b) First Option Period: One (1) Album containing a minimum of ten (10) Master Recordings
    (c) Subsequent Option Periods: One (1) Album containing a minimum of ten (10) Master Recordings each

2.2 Recording Procedure. All Master Recordings shall be produced and recorded at recording studios approved by Label, under the supervision of a producer approved by Label, using arrangements and musicians approved by Label.

2.3 Delivery Requirements. Each Album shall be delivered in a form that is technically satisfactory for commercial release, including all necessary clearances, licenses, and permissions.

ARTICLE 3: ADVANCES

3.1 Signing Advance. Upon execution of this Agreement, Label shall pay Artist a non-refundable but recoupable advance in the amount of Seventy-Five Thousand Dollars ($75,000.00).

3.2 Album Advances. Label shall pay Artist the following advances upon commencement of recording for each Album:

    (a) First Album (Initial Period): $75,000 (included in signing advance)
    (b) Second Album (First Option Period): $125,000
    (c) Third Album (Second Option Period): $175,000
    (d) Fourth Album (Third Option Period): $225,000
    (e) Fifth Album (Fourth Option Period): $300,000

3.3 Nature of Advances. All advances are non-refundable but fully recoupable from any and all royalties otherwise payable to Artist under this Agreement.

ARTICLE 4: ROYALTIES

4.1 Basic Royalty Rate. Subject to the provisions of this Agreement, Label shall pay Artist royalties computed at the following percentages of the applicable Royalty Base Price:

    (a) Physical Records (CDs, Vinyl): Fourteen percent (14%) of PPD
    (b) Digital Permanent Downloads: Sixteen percent (16%) of PPD
    (c) Streaming: Fifty percent (50%) of Label's Net Receipts
    (d) Synchronization Licenses: Fifty percent (50%) of Net Sync Fees

4.2 Reduced Royalties. Notwithstanding the foregoing, royalties shall be reduced as follows:

    (a) Foreign Sales: 75% of otherwise applicable rate
    (b) Budget Records: 50% of otherwise applicable rate
    (c) Record Club Sales: 50% of otherwise applicable rate
    (d) Compilations: Pro-rata share based on number of tracks

4.3 Reserves. Label may withhold a reasonable reserve against returns not to exceed thirty-five percent (35%) of royalties otherwise payable, which reserves shall be liquidated within four (4) accounting periods.

ARTICLE 5: CONTROLLED COMPOSITIONS

5.1 Mechanical License. Artist hereby grants to Label a mechanical license for all Controlled Compositions at seventy-five percent (75%) of the minimum statutory rate in effect as of the date of delivery of the applicable Master Recording.

5.2 Maximum Aggregate Rate. The maximum aggregate mechanical royalty rate payable by Label for any Album shall not exceed ten (10) times the applicable Controlled Composition rate, regardless of the actual number of compositions embodied thereon.

5.3 Excess Mechanical Royalties. In the event that the aggregate mechanical royalties payable for any Album exceed the maximum aggregate rate, such excess shall be deducted from Artist's royalties.

ARTICLE 6: RECOUPMENT

6.1 Recoupable Costs. The following costs shall be charged against and recoupable from Artist's royalties:

    (a) All recording costs, including studio time, producer fees, musician fees, engineering, mixing, and mastering
    (b) One hundred percent (100%) of all Advances
    (c) Fifty percent (50%) of the costs of producing music videos
    (d) Fifty percent (50%) of independent radio promotion costs
    (e) One hundred percent (100%) of tour support
    (f) Twenty-five percent (25%) of marketing and promotional costs in excess of $50,000 per Album

6.2 Cross-Collateralization. All advances, recording costs, and other recoupable charges under this Agreement may be cross-collateralized across all Albums delivered hereunder.

ARTICLE 7: GRANT OF RIGHTS

7.1 Master Recording Ownership. All Master Recordings made during the Term, together with all performances embodied therein, shall be entirely Label's property, free from any claims by Artist or any person deriving rights from Artist. Label shall have the exclusive worldwide right in perpetuity to manufacture, distribute, sell, and otherwise exploit such Master Recordings.

7.2 Name and Likeness. Artist grants to Label the perpetual right to use and permit others to use Artist's name (both legal and professional), likeness, photograph, biographical material, and other identification for purposes of advertising, promotion, and trade in connection with Artist's recordings.

7.3 Work for Hire. All Master Recordings shall be considered "works made for hire" under the United States Copyright Act. To the extent any Master Recording is not deemed a work made for hire, Artist hereby irrevocably assigns to Label all right, title, and interest in and to such Master Recording, including the copyright therein.

ARTICLE 8: EXCLUSIVITY

8.1 Exclusive Services. During the Term, Artist's recording services shall be exclusive to Label. Artist shall not perform for the purpose of making recordings for any person or entity other than Label.

8.2 Re-Recording Restriction. Artist shall not re-record any composition recorded hereunder for any person or entity other than Label for a period of five (5) years following the later of: (a) the date of recording, or (b) the expiration or termination of the Term.

8.3 Sideman Performances. Notwithstanding the foregoing, Artist may perform as a non-featured sideman on recordings by other artists, provided that: (a) Artist receives no credit larger than that accorded other sidemen; (b) Label receives prior written notice; and (c) such performances do not interfere with Artist's obligations hereunder.

ARTICLE 9: ARTIST WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS

9.1 Artist warrants and represents that:

    (a) Artist has the full right and authority to enter into this Agreement
    (b) Artist is not subject to any prior agreements that would interfere with the performance of this Agreement
    (c) All compositions written by Artist and recorded hereunder shall be original and shall not infringe upon the rights of any third party
    (d) Artist shall comply with all applicable union agreements and requirements

ARTICLE 10: LABEL OBLIGATIONS

10.1 Release Commitment. Label agrees to release commercially at least one (1) Album during each contract period, subject to receipt of commercially satisfactory Master Recordings.

10.2 Marketing Support. Label shall use commercially reasonable efforts to promote and market each Album, provided that the specific nature and extent of such efforts shall be in Label's sole discretion.

10.3 Accounting. Label shall render accounting statements to Artist within ninety (90) days following the end of each calendar quarter, accompanied by payment of any royalties shown to be due.

ARTICLE 11: AUDIT RIGHTS

11.1 Artist shall have the right, upon reasonable written notice, to audit Label's books and records relating to this Agreement once per calendar year. Such audit shall be conducted during normal business hours by a certified public accountant and shall be at Artist's expense unless discrepancies of ten percent (10%) or more are discovered, in which case Label shall reimburse Artist's audit costs.

ARTICLE 12: TERMINATION

12.1 Label's Right to Terminate. Label may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to Artist if:

    (a) Artist materially breaches any provision hereof and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice
    (b) Artist becomes unable to perform due to death, disability, or incarceration
    (c) Any Album fails to achieve sales of at least 50,000 units within twelve (12) months of release

12.2 Effect of Termination. Upon termination, all rights granted to Label in Master Recordings delivered prior to termination shall survive, and Label shall have no further obligation to pay advances or record additional Albums.

ARTICLE 13: RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL

13.1 Following the expiration or termination of this Agreement, Artist shall not enter into any recording agreement with any third party without first offering Label the opportunity to match such third party's terms. Artist shall provide Label with written notice of any bona fide third party offer, and Label shall have thirty (30) days to exercise its right of first refusal.

ARTICLE 14: MISCELLANEOUS

14.1 Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.

14.2 Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements relating to the subject matter hereof.

14.3 Assignment. Label may assign this Agreement to any party. Artist may not assign this Agreement without Label's prior written consent.

14.4 Notices. All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses set forth above.
