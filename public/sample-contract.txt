SONGWRITER PUBLISHING AGREEMENT

This Songwriter Publishing Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2025 (the "Effective Date").

PARTIES:
Publisher: Lyric House LLC ("Publisher")
Writer: John Smith ("Writer")

1. GRANT OF RIGHTS

Writer hereby sells, assigns and transfers to Publisher for a consecutive period of five (5) years 100% ownership interest/administration of the "publishing" rights in the Works (as defined below), including the worldwide copyrights therein. Writer retains the writer's share and authorship of the Work.

2. TERM AND TERRITORY

The term of this Agreement shall be five (5) years from the Effective Date with automatic one-year extensions unless terminated by either party with 90 days written notice. Territory shall be Worldwide.

3. WORKS

"Works" means all musical compositions and/or original lyrics written or co-written by Writer during Sessions arranged by Publisher during the Term.

4. REVENUE AND COMPENSATION

Publisher shall pay Writer fifty percent (50%) of all net sums actually received by Publisher from exploitation of the Works. Payments shall be made semi-annually on September 30 and March 31 of each year. No payment will be made until the amount due exceeds $100.

5. SYNC LICENSING

Publisher shall use best efforts to pitch Works for synchronization licensing opportunities including film, television, advertising, and video games. Publisher retains the right to negotiate and execute sync licenses on Writer's behalf.

6. POST-TERM COLLECTION

Publisher shall retain the right to continue collecting revenue from any placements that resulted from pitches made during the Term, for a period of 2 years post-termination.

7. INDEMNIFICATION

Writer agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Publisher from any and all claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising from any breach of Writer's warranties or representations under this Agreement.

8. AUDIT RIGHTS

Writer shall have the right, upon thirty (30) days written notice and during normal business hours, to audit Publisher's books and records relating to the Works.

9. ASSIGNMENT

Publisher shall have the right to assign this Agreement to any affiliated company or to any successor in interest without Writer's consent.

10. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement upon material breach by the other party, provided that the breaching party fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice.
